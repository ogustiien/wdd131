Our Vision

Driving through Japan’s mountain passes—the legendary touge—in true MF Ghost style has always been the ultimate dream. There is a visceral pull to the idea of navigating the Hakone Turnpike or the Irohazaka slopes, hearing the high-revving echo of an engine against the rock walls. It’s about the mechanical purity and the "New Rich" versus "Old School" spirit found in Shuichi Shigeno’s universe. To perfectly clip an apex while the cherry blossoms blur past isn’t just about speed; it’s about that synchronized dance between man and machine in the birthplace of drifting culture..
