Website Project Plan

Site Name & Branding

Site Name:JDM - Japan Driven Mountain

Description:The name Japanese Driven Mountains was selected to evoke a specific intersection of automotive culture and geography. In the car enthusiast community, "Japanese Driven" refers to the legendary engineering and tuning heritage of JDM (Japanese Domestic Market) vehicles. Combining this with "Mountains" directly references the touge (mountain pass) roads where this driving culture was born and perfected. This name and its borrowed abreviation identifies the site as a niche destination for fans of Japanese performance cars and the scenic, technical driving routes associated with them.

Domain (Optional): https://jdm.com

Site Purpose

This site provides a scenic getaway for enthusiasts of japanese car culture and car culture as a whole to meet together, race, tandem drift, or to just drive cars the fast way down the mountain.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Where can i rent and race JDM vehicles?
  • Scenario 2: What are the most iconic mountain passes in the Gunma prefecture for a weekend drive?
